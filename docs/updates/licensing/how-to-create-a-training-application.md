## `How to Create a Training Application in Plant an App`

This article provides a step-by-step guide on how to create a training application.

## `My Apps Page`

Make sure you are on the **My Apps** page and that the **My Live Apps** tab is selected, then click the **New App** button.
<img src="/img/new-app-plant-an-app-my-apps.png" alt="new-app-plant-an-app-my-apps.png" ></img>

The next step is to fill in the required fields in the form that appears and select **Training Environment** from the subscription dropdown. To create a training application, your organization must first be assigned a training subscription. If the training subscription is not visible in your account, please <a href="https://console.plantanapp.com/support">contact us</a>.
<img src="/img/new-app-plant-an-app-my-apps-form.png" alt="new-app-plant-an-app-my-apps-form.png" ></img>

Next, press **Create** and wait until your application is deployed.
<img src="/img/new-app-plant-an-app-my-apps-create-new-provisioning.png" alt="new-app-plant-an-app-my-apps-create-new-provisioning.png" ></img>

After a few minutes, you should see that the app URL has changed, and you can now log in using the username and password you provided in the previous step.
<img src="/img/new-app-plant-an-app-my-apps-app-ready.png" alt="new-app-plant-an-app-my-apps-app-ready.png" ></img>

You will also receive a confirmation email.
<img src="/img/my-apps-confirmation-mail.png" alt="my-apps-confirmation-mail.png" ></img>

Access the URL and enter your credentials:
<img src="/img/login-screen-password-and-username.png" alt="login-screen-password-and-username.png" ></img>

After logging in, you will see another confirmation indicating that you are using a **training environment**. Be aware that the **training environment** should not be used as a **production environment**.

Press the **Start** button.
<img src="/img/my-apps-plant-an-app-confirmation-non-production-environment.png" alt="my-apps-plant-an-app-confirmation-non-production-environment.png" ></img>

In the next step, simply press **Start from Scratch**.
<img src="/img/my-apps-start-from-scratch-1582644217.png" alt="my-apps-start-from-scratch-1582644217.png" ></img>

You will now see the intro screen.
<img src="/img/you-can-follow-the-guide.png" alt="you-can-follow-the-guide.png" ></img>

Press **Begin Guided Activity** to learn the basics of Plant an App.
<img src="/img/follow-the-guide-screen.png" alt="follow-the-guide-screen.png" ></img>

Now follow the instructions and press **Configuration**. When you try to access the configuration page for the first time, you will need to activate a developer seat.

To do that, simply press **Activate**.
<img src="/img/activate-your-development-seat.png" alt="activate-your-development-seat.png" ></img>

You should now see your organization. Press **Activate** again to activate a developer seat.
<img src="/img/my-apps-activate-dev.png" alt="my-apps-activate-dev.png" ></img>

You will now be redirected to the configuration page, and the guide will continue with the entities. Enjoy using Plant an App!
<img src="/img/enjoy!.png" alt="enjoy!.png" ></img>
